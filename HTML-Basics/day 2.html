SUMMER ALKALIZING SALAD

Ingredients:

    butter lettuce
  • a handful of arugula
  • 2 raspberry tomatoes or other favorite
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 medium-sized cucumber
  • 3 radishes
  • a handful of sunflower seeds
  • olive oil or other cold-pressed vegetable oil
  • salt and other favorite spices to taste

Execution:

  1. all vegetables thoroughly, peel the avocado and cucumber.
  2. Dice the tomatoes, avocado, cucumber and radishes.
  3. Tear the butter lettuce and arugula into smaller pieces.
  4. the sunflower seeds in a frying pan.
  5. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  6. Add the spices and olive oil, then mix the salad again.

The salad is now ready to eat. Isn't it very quick and easy? In addition, the dish is a treasure trove of vitamins, minerals and beneficial fatty acids, so it is worth eating it often.
